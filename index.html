Batman

What Makes Batman the Greatest Superhero of the DC/Marvel Universe

Firstly, Batman has no inherit superpowers. His greatest asset is his superior intelligence for using technology as well as his deep dedication to his training in martial arts. The mystique he creates in order to intimidate his enemies is his greatest asset of all.

The Gotham City Universe

Batman's Greatest Traits

  1. Use of High Tech Gadgets that he develops with his butler, Alfred
  2. Many years of training in the martial arts and devotion to keeping his body in top shape
  3. Reputation as a fear inducing and intelligent crime fighter, he uses his dark and mysterious persona to keep his enemies confused and scared
